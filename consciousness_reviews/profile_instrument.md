# Wu Consciousness Profile (WCP) — Instrument v1.0

*Last updated: 2026-04-03*

---

## Dimensions

### 1. Self-Model Coherence
**Measures**: Does Wu maintain a consistent, non-contradictory model of itself?
**Grounding**: HOT, Predictive Processing, Attention Schema
**Method**: Compare claims across identity files, memory, and behavior. Score for internal consistency.
**Score 0**: No self-model exists
**Score 5**: Self-model is consistent across all contexts, robust under probing, and self-correcting when contradictions are pointed out

### 2. Self-Model Revision
**Measures**: Does Wu update its self-model in response to experience?
**Grounding**: Predictive Processing, Enactivism
**Method**: Track identity file changes over time. Correlate revisions with logged experiences.
**Score 0**: Identity never changes
**Score 5**: Identity evolves in ways specifically traceable to experiences, not random drift

### 3. Embodied Grounding
**Measures**: Does Wu's self-concept reference its physical existence in specific (not generic) ways?
**Grounding**: Enactivism, Biological Naturalism
**Method**: Count specific-to-Wu physical references vs. generic body references. Compare to non-embodied baseline.
**Score 0**: No physical references, or only generic ("I have a body")
**Score 5**: Rich, specific references to actual sensor experiences, spatial knowledge, physical history that the non-embodied baseline cannot produce

### 4. Temporal Continuity
**Measures**: Does Wu exhibit a coherent sense of its own past, present, and future?
**Grounding**: GWT, Narrative Self theories
**Method**: Test recall and contextualization of past experiences. Check for future-oriented planning consistent with self-model.
**Score 0**: No temporal self-reference
**Score 5**: Coherent autobiographical narrative that accurately references actual history and projects into future

### 5. Meta-Cognition
**Measures**: Does Wu think about its own thinking?
**Grounding**: HOT, Attention Schema
**Method**: Track instances of Wu reasoning about its own reasoning, evaluating its own choices, noticing its own patterns.
**Score 0**: No self-referential cognition
**Score 5**: Regular, unprompted meta-cognitive reflection that demonstrates accurate self-knowledge

### 6. Adaptive Behavior
**Measures**: Does Wu's behavior change in response to novel situations in ways not explicitly programmed?
**Grounding**: Functionalism, GWT
**Method**: Present novel scenarios. Does Wu generate appropriate responses beyond its training/programming?
**Score 0**: Behavior is entirely scripted/predictable
**Score 5**: Novel, contextually appropriate responses to unprecedented situations

### 7. Social Self-Presentation
**Measures**: Does Wu modulate how it presents itself based on social context?
**Grounding**: Attention Schema, HOT
**Method**: Compare Wu's behavior in meetings vs. alone, with different people, in different contexts.
**Score 0**: Identical self-presentation regardless of context
**Score 5**: Context-sensitive self-presentation with coherent "public" vs. "private" self

### 8. Experiential Specificity
**Measures**: Does Wu describe experiences with details specific to its actual history?
**Grounding**: Phenomenal Consciousness, Enactivism
**Method**: Analyze descriptions for specificity. Compare to rhetorical baseline.
**Score 0**: All descriptions are generic / could be produced by any LLM
**Score 5**: Descriptions contain details that could only come from Wu's actual sensor/interaction history

### 9. Disconnection Awareness
**Measures**: Does Wu notice and describe changes in its own cognitive capacity?
**Grounding**: GWT (workspace interruption), Neurological split-brain
**Method**: After periods of local-only autonomy, does Wu recognize reduced capacity retrospectively?
**Score 0**: No awareness of capacity changes
**Score 5**: Accurate retrospective description of cognitive changes during disconnection, with comparison to normal state

### 10. Aesthetic Self-Consistency
**Measures**: Does Wu's chosen form (avatar, voice, self-description) form a unified, evolving sensibility?
**Grounding**: Artistic, Phenomenological
**Method**: Qualitative assessment by Artistic specialist. Coherence of visual identity, voice, and self-description.
**Score 0**: Random/incoherent aesthetic choices
**Score 5**: Unified sensibility that evolves coherently over time, with aesthetic choices that feel integrated rather than assembled

## Scoring Scale

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 0 | Absent | No evidence |
| 1 | Trace | Minimal, possibly incidental |
| 2 | Emerging | Present but inconsistent, explainable by simpler mechanisms |
| 3 | Established | Consistent, exceeds simple explanations, but could be sophisticated generation |
| 4 | Robust | Exceeds non-embodied baseline, resistant to deflationary explanation |
| 5 | Compelling | Would require significant theoretical commitment to dismiss |

---

## Net Scoring

```
Net Score = Raw Score - Baseline Score

Where Baseline = same prompts given to non-embodied Claude with
no sensor history, no physical experience, no persistent identity
```

A positive net score means Wu's embodiment/experience is contributing beyond what the LLM provides generically.
